Best of Us

A worksheet to help you understand your strengths, name your needs, and communicate how you work best. Use your Bring/Need Report and Personal Insights Report alongside each step.

Step 1 of 4

Understanding What Helps You Thrive

Use the Bring/Need Report to understand how each of your top strengths contributes to enhancing situations. Look at the "I bring" section, which lists the value you add through each strength. This will guide you in identifying the environments and activities that allow you to thrive.

Use: Bring/Need Report
Look at the "I Bring" section — the value you add through each strength.
Step 2 of 4

Identifying What Might Hold You Back

The "I need" section of the Bring/Need Report shows the specific conditions or needs that must be met for your strengths to flourish. If these needs are unmet, they may become barriers to success. Use this to identify what could potentially hold you back and think about how to address these needs.

Use: Bring/Need Report
Look at the "I Need" section — specific conditions required for your strengths to flourish.
Step 3 of 4

Knowing How Others Can Rely on You

In the Personal Insights Report, focus on the section that describes "I am (being)" and "I will (doing)." These will help you define the unique ways others can count on you and what you consistently bring to the table. Use this to clearly communicate how others can rely on you, based on your strengths.

Use: Personal Insights Report
Look at "I am (Being)" and "I will (Doing)" — the unique ways others can count on you.
Step 4 of 4

Clearly Communicating Your Needs

Look at the "I love" (motivators) and "I dislike" (demotivators) sections in the Personal Insights Report. These areas highlight what energises and demotivates you. Use this to clearly communicate your needs to others, ensuring they understand what environment and conditions allow you to work at your best.

Use: Personal Insights Report
Look at "I love (Motivators)" and "I dislike (Demotivators)" — what energises or detracts from your performance.

Key Reports Reference

1. Bring/Need Report
I Bring: Understand how you add value in multiple ways, based on your strengths.
I Need: Identify the specific needs required for each strength to thrive.
2. Personal Insights Report
I am (Being): Describes who you are and how your strengths shape your behaviour.
I will (Doing): Outlines how you put your strengths into action.
I love (Motivators): What energises you and fuels your performance.
I dislike (Demotivators): What detracts from your motivation and performance.

By using these reports to complete the 'Best of Us' worksheet, you will gain a comprehensive understanding of how your strengths help you thrive, identify potential challenges, clarify how others can count on you, and articulate your needs for success.
