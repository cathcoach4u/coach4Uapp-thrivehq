Become a Body Doubling Host

Would you like a built-in reason to stay focused—while helping others do the same?

You don’t need to facilitate or lead. You just need to show up, open the room, and work alongside others. It’s one of the most rewarding things you can do — for yourself and the community.

What hosting gives you

🗓️ Your schedule, your choice Pick a time that fits — you’re in full control of when it runs.
🎯 A built-in reason to focus Hosting gives you accountability too — you’ll get things done alongside the group.
🤝 Real connection A genuine way to build momentum with others in the ThriveHQ community.
✨ Quiet satisfaction Knowing your presence helped someone else get things done is a powerful feeling.

Your role as host

✅ Confirm the day before Let the community know you’ll be there and you’re ready to host.
💻 Log in and get to work Show up at the scheduled time and commit to an hour of focused work alongside the group.
