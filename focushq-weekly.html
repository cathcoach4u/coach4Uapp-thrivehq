Weekly Intention

This Week's Intention
What do you want this week to be about?

Your weekly intention isn't a to-do list — it's a direction. A short statement that helps you make decisions, prioritise, and feel a sense of purpose across the week.

Think: what would make this week feel worthwhile?

How to use it
  • Set it at the start of the week — Sunday evening or Monday morning works well.
  • When you're unsure what to do next, ask: does this fit my intention?
  • At the end of the week, reflect on whether you lived it out.
  • Your intention resets automatically each week.
