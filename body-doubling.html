Interested in Hosting?

Hosting a Body Doubling session is one of the most rewarding things you can do — for yourself and for the community. You don't need to facilitate or lead; you just need to show up, open the room, and work alongside others.

What hosting gives you

  • Choose a time that fits your schedule — you're in control of when it runs
  • A built-in reason to sit down and focus — hosting keeps you on task too
  • A genuine way to connect with others in the group and build momentum together
  • The quiet satisfaction of knowing your presence helped someone else get things done

Your role as host

  • Confirm the day before that you're available and ready to host
  • Log in at the scheduled time and commit to an hour of focused work alongside the group
