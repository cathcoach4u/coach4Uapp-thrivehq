Today's Focus

Your Most Important Task
What is the one thing you need to get done today?

Your MIT (Most Important Task) is the task that, if completed, makes today a win — even if nothing else gets done. Choose one. Not a list.

Think: what would I regret leaving undone?

Why it works

Most productivity problems aren't about doing too little — they're about not deciding what matters most. Naming your MIT at the start of the day cuts through the noise and gives you a clear target. When the day gets messy, you always know what to return to.
